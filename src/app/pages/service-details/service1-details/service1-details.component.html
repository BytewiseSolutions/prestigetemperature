Technology That Gives You Total Control

VRV systems (Variable Refrigerant Volume) are only suitable for very large homes and commercial projects. If you're looking to heat massive spaces the VRV system could be just what you're looking for. Daikin VRV outdoor units can be easily located on the roof avoiding the need for a plant room, and installation can be carried out one floor at a time. Independent control of each unit avoids energy waste and a wide range of indoor unit options provides great flexibility for dwellers.

Key Benefits of VRV Systems

  • Energy Efficiency: One of the standout features of VRV systems is their high energy efficiency. By adjusting the refrigerant flow to match the exact heating requirements of each zone, VRV systems avoid the energy losses associated with traditional fixed-speed heating systems. This results in significant energy savings and lower utility bills.

  • Flexible Design: VRV systems offer unparalleled design flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of building types and sizes. The modular nature of these systems allows for easy expansion or reconfiguration, accommodating changes in building use or occupancy without major modifications.

  • Zoning Capabilities:: With VRV systems, users can create multiple heating zones within a building, each with its own temperature settings. This zoned approach not only enhances comfort by allowing personalized temperature control but also contributes to energy savings by heating only the occupied areas.

  • Quiet Operation: VRV systems are known for their quiet operation, both indoors and outdoors. The use of advanced compressor technology and sound-dampening materials ensures that these systems operate at low noise levels, making them ideal for noise-sensitive environments such as offices, hotels, and residential buildings.

  • Advanced Control Options: Modern VRV systems come equipped with advanced control options, including remote control, smartphone apps, and integration with building management systems. These controls provide users with convenient and precise management of their heating systems, further enhancing energy efficiency and comfort.

  • Environmental Benefits: By using refrigerants with lower global warming potential (GWP) and offering high energy efficiency, VRV systems contribute to reduced greenhouse gas emissions. Additionally, the ability to recover and reuse heat within the system makes VRV an environmentally friendly choice for heating and cooling.
